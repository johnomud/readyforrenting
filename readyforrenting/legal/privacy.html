Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 April 2026

Who we are

Ready for Renting ("we", "us") is a UK-based business that provides compliance documents and certificate-tracking tools for private landlords in England. Our contact email is help@readyforrenting.uk.

What we collect

We collect only what is necessary to deliver the service you have paid for:

  • Email address — provided by Stripe when you purchase. Used to deliver your documents, send certificate reminders, and respond to support queries.
  • Property and certificate data — entered by you in the Certificate Tracker. Stored in our Notion-hosted database and used solely to generate your reminders.
  • Payment data — handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card number.

How we use your data

We use your data to fulfil your purchase, send certificate deadline reminders, and respond to support requests. We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party processors

We use the following services to operate:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Notion — secure data storage for tracker records
  • Vercel — website hosting
  • Cloudflare — DNS and security

Each processor has its own privacy policy and processes data under contract.

Cookies

We use a single functional cookie to remember your dark/light theme preference. We do not use analytics cookies, advertising trackers, or any third-party tracking scripts.

Data retention

We retain your email and tracker data for as long as your subscription is active, plus 90 days after cancellation. Document Pack purchase records are retained for accounting and tax purposes as required by HMRC (typically 6 years). You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, delete or export your personal data. You also have the right to object to processing or withdraw consent. To exercise any of these rights, email help@readyforrenting.uk and we will respond within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated by email to active customers. The "last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the current version.

Refund Policy

Last updated: 20 April 2026

Document Pack — 30-day money-back guarantee

If you're not happy with the Document Pack for any reason, email us within 30 days of purchase and we'll refund you in full, no questions asked. This goes beyond the 14-day cooling-off period required by UK consumer law — because we'd rather you felt confident buying.

Certificate Tracker — cancel anytime

You can cancel your Tracker subscription at any time. When you cancel:

  • You keep access until the end of your current billing period (monthly or annual).
  • No further charges are taken.
  • Your tracker data remains available to export for 90 days after cancellation.

If you cancel within the first 14 days of a new subscription, we'll refund the full amount. After 14 days, we offer a pro-rata refund for any unused full months remaining on an annual plan.

How to request a refund

Email help@readyforrenting.uk with your purchase email address. We aim to process all refunds within 5 working days. Refunds are returned to the original payment method via Stripe.

Your statutory rights

Nothing in this policy affects your statutory rights under the Consumer Rights Act 2015 or the Consumer Contracts (Information, Cancellation and Additional Charges) Regulations 2013. As a UK consumer purchasing digital content, you have a statutory 14-day right to cancel from the date of purchase.
